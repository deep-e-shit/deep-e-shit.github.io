Starting Something New—An Inspiration

By: Deepesh
March 27, 2025 - 03:45

I was talking to my girlfriend tonight about random shit on relationships while she was working on her blogging site. She’s been doing this for a long time, writing consistently, putting her thoughts out there, and somehow staying committed to it. And that got me thinking—why shouldn’t I write some blogs too?

I’ve thought about doing something like this for a long time. Writing has always been one of those things in the back of my mind, an itch I never quite scratched. But tonight, something clicked. Maybe it was the way she was so focused on her blog, or maybe it was just one of those moments where an idea sticks instead of slipping away. Either way, here I am, writing my first blog post.

Now, here’s the catch—I have ADHD. And if you know anything about ADHD, you know that starting new things is easy. The rush of a fresh idea? Chef’s kiss. The excitement of a new hobby? Unmatched. The follow-through? Eh, not so much. My life is basically a collection of unfinished hobbies and half-baked ideas that never quite made it past the honeymoon phase. So why not add blogging to the list?

But what if this time is different? What if I actually keep going? What if, instead of letting this become another abandoned project, I use it as a space to embrace my chaotic mind and just… write? No pressure, no expectations—just thoughts spilled onto a screen, however messy they may be.

So, here’s to starting something new. Again. Let’s see where this one goes.
